ABOUT

I am a UX Designer with expert knowledge in Web Design, Product Marketing, and Client Success. My experience in cross-departmental, user-facing projects gives my work a holistic, user-centered perspective.

My specialties include User-Centered Web Design, Interaction Design, Content Creation, and Branding. The design tools I rely on are Sketch, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

My knowledge of HTML and CSS make me a practical team member in an agile setting. I love incorporating research and data into my projects to create solutions that inspire both internal teams and users.
