Hi, I'm Allison

I'm a user experience designer at Captricity. Although I am a UX generalist, my favorite projects include conducting user research to properly define the problems I'm solving.

I view myself as a user advocate above all else, campaigning for their perspective to be represented in every design decision. I'm also a strong believer in applying internal, cross-departmental communication to the design process.

As far as tools go, I think Sketch is the absolute best. I take a flexible stance regarding other tools - although I use Invision most often for prototyping. Invision, Intercom, Julie Zhuo, and Nick Babich are my favorite sources for design thought leadership.

When I’m not fussing over users, I’m out in the mountains or paddleboarding on the water.
