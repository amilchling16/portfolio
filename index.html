ABOUT

I am a UX Designer with a background in Web Design, Product Marketing, and Client Success. My experience in cross-departmental and user-facing projects gives my work a holistic, user-centered perspective. My specialties include Interaction Design, Wireframing, User-Centered Website Design, and Branding. The design tools I rely on are Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, and sketching in my notebook.

My knowledge of HTML and CSS make me a practical team member in an agile setting. I love incorporating research and data into my design process to create visual solutions that inspire both internal teams and users/clients.
