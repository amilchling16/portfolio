APP UX DESIGN

USER ANALYSIS

I audited and organized thousands of customer support queries. Most issues were rooted in a misunderstanding of the interaction of the two main features: A) choosing distracting sites to blocks and then B) scheduling lengths of time for them to be inaccessible.

PERSONAS

I held phone interviews with our top ten users and corresponded with users through the support site. I was able to develop our two largest use cases with my analytics and personal interactions.

HEURISTIC EVALUATION

How did the user data match up? I analyzed our current app interface to see where we were missing the mark.

ALIGNMENT

I presented my previous findings to the team to champion their support. Prioritization was discussed by remaining true to our mission statement and the key services we provide. I received feedback to refine my observations into a unified agenda for our team.

REQUIREMENTS

After establishing team buyin, I made a list of our core requirements. I transformed the list into concepts and began iterating on design solutions. Our must haves in the new design:

  • Stronger connection between making block list and creating a schedule
  • Expanded curated filter list
  • Clearer form for custom URL entry
  • Consolidated scheduler
  • A walkthrough of how the product works
  • Differentiated and emphasized “block everything” setting
  • A less text-reliant interface for free version
